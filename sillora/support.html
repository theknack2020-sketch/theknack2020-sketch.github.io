Sillora — the honest, on-device wardrobe for people who love fragrance.

How do I add a bottle?

Tap the + on the Wardrobe screen. Start typing a name and pick a match to auto-fill the house, concentration, and notes — or fill in anything by hand. A missing catalog entry never stops you from adding a bottle.

How do I log my Scent of the Day?

On the Today tab, tap a bottle in the quick-pick strip — that's it. You can also log from the Home Screen widget or by asking Siri ("Log my scent in Sillora").

What is cost-per-wear?

It's what a bottle has cost you each time you've worn it — the price you paid divided by the number of wears. A $150 bottle worn 60 times costs $2.50 a wear.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → export as CSV or JSON. It's free and available anytime. Your collection is never held hostage.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the Sillora Pro screen and tap "Restore Purchases", or reinstall the app while signed in to the same Apple ID.

How does iCloud sync work?

Sillora Pro can sync your collection across your devices using your own private iCloud. It's off by default; the app stays fully local until you turn it on.

How do I turn off reminders?

Settings inside Sillora, or iPhone Settings → Sillora → Notifications.
