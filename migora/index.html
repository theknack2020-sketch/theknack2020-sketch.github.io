What it is

Migora is a migraine journal built around three moments: the seconds when an attack starts, the days when you want to understand why, and the appointment where you finally have the full picture. Log an attack in under ten seconds with Attack Mode — true-black, large targets, zero typing. Watch how your recorded attacks line up with local barometric pressure. Track sleep, triggers, medications, and daily check-ins. And when you see your clinician, generate a clean report from everything you've logged. No account, everything on your device, and your health data is yours alone.

Features

Attack Mode

Log an attack in seconds — large targets, no typing, easy on light-sensitive eyes.

Pressure & patterns

See how your attacks line up with local barometric pressure and your own triggers.

Daily check-ins

A ten-second note on sleep, stress, hydration, and mood — the context behind the pattern.

Doctor report

Generate a clear PDF — heat map, frequency, medication-use days, and MIDAS trend.

Private by design

No account, no tracking, no ads. Your health data stays on your device.

Apple-native

Widgets, Live Activity, Siri, dark mode, and gentle, pressure-free reminders.

Migora is a tracking tool, not a medical device. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a healthcare professional.
