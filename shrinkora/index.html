Shrinkora

Shrink it. Send it. It never leaves your phone.

The clean, on-device video compressor. Pick a video, pick where it's going, and Shrinkora makes it fit — with no ads, no tracking, and no uploads.

  • Made to fit — WhatsApp, iMessage, Email, Discord, or a custom megabyte target
  • Proof before you commit — see the result size and a quality preview first
  • On-device only — your video is compressed by your phone's hardware encoder
  • No photo-library access — the system picker hands over just the one video
  • No network — the app has no networking code, so nothing can be uploaded
  • Batch mode and custom targets in Shrinkora Pro; 3 free compressions to start
