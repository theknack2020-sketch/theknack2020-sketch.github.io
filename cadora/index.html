What it is

Cadora is a gift planner for iPhone and iPad built around one idea: thoughtful gifting is remembering. Keep a page for each person you give to, track their birthdays and anniversaries, jot gift ideas as they come to you, follow each idea from thought to given, and set a budget — so the right gift is ready before the date arrives. It's giver-first and evergreen: not a wishlist, not a Christmas-only app. Everything lives on your device and, if you choose, syncs privately through your own iCloud.

Features

People you give to

A calm page per person — relationship, interests, sizes, notes, and their gift history.

Occasions, all year

Birthdays, anniversaries, and recurring dates, with a clear countdown to what's next.

Gift ideas & status

Capture ideas anytime and follow each one from idea to ordered, wrapped, and given.

Budget & history

Track spend per person and per year, and never accidentally repeat a gift.

Private by design

No account with us, no tracking, no ads. Your data stays on device and in your own iCloud.

Made for iPhone & iPad

A warm, editorial design with dark mode, optional Face ID lock, and gentle reminders.
