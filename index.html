What it is

Selfora is a daily affirmation app for iPhone and iPad built around one idea: the words you repeat become the person you become. Swipe a calm full-screen feed of "I am" affirmations, favorite the ones that land, and build a gentle daily practice with a short breath-and-repetition session. Everything lives on your device, syncs through your own private iCloud, and never asks for an account.

Features

Daily affirmations

A calm full-screen feed across 20 categories, from Self-Love to New Beginnings.

Practice & streak

A 1–2 minute breath-and-repetition session. The habit engine is always free.

Home & Lock widgets

Affirmations rotate on your Home and Lock Screen throughout the day.

Private by design

No account, no tracking, no ads. Your data stays on your device.

Write your own

Add affirmations in your own voice and see them in your feed and widgets.

Made for iPhone & iPad

A universal app with sunrise themes, dark mode, and a full-canvas iPad layout.
