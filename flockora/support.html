Frequently asked

How do I log eggs?

Tap the brass tally drum on the Coop tab to add one egg. Touch and hold it to enter a specific number or make a correction. You can also add the Flockora widget to your Home Screen and log with a single tap.

What is cost per egg?

Flockora divides everything you've spent — feed, bedding, supplements, equipment — by every egg you've collected, so you can see what your "free" eggs really cost and when you break even against the store price. Set your local store price for a dozen in Settings.

How does Hatch Day work?

Start a hatch from the Hatch tab: set the egg count, start date, and species. Flockora lays out the 21-day map (or the right length for ducks, quail, turkeys, and geese), reminds you to candle and lock down, and — with Flockora Pro — shows a live countdown on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

What's included in Flockora Pro?

Egg Economics with a share card, the Hatch Day Live Activity, weather advisories for heat and frost, CSV and PDF export, and unlimited insights history. Egg logging, widgets, hen profiles, photos, health records, and hatch tracking are always free.

How do I restore a purchase?

Go to Settings inside Flockora and tap "Restore purchases". Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple Account you used to buy Pro.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device. Flockora has no account and collects no analytics. See our Privacy Policy.

Can Flockora diagnose a sick hen?

No. Flockora lets you record what you observe, but it does not give veterinary advice. For a sick bird, please consult a veterinarian.
